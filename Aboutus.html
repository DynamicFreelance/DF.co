About Us

Dynamic Technologies

Founded on April 6th 2023 by Adrian Ubani & Christian Vala, Dynamic Technologies is an entrepreneurs dream. We remove the hastle of website development for businesses of all scales, design eye catching logos, and ensure that your clients have a smooth elegant online experience when working with your business. The Dynamic Technologies staff consists of highly trained and experienced professionals, in order to produce the highest quality digital branding our customers and yours, could ever imagine.

Our Co-Founders

Adrian Ubani

When I first met my co-founder and business partner Christian, I was a dreamer filled with creative ideas. Christian and I share many similar hobbies, together we were able to bring many ideas to life. We designed and produced our first game after I had brought up the concept, and he created the code.
